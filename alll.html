"All Of Me" is a song by American recording artist John Legend from his fourth studio album Love in the Future (2013). Legend co-wrote the song with Toby Gad and co-produced it with Dave Tozer.
The song was described as an R&B ballad and "tender piano ballad". "

All Of Me" was the first Hot 100 No. 1 single of Legend's career. It also reached No. 1 in other countries such as Australia, Belgium, Canada, Ireland, The Netherlands, Portugal, Sweden and Switzerland.
